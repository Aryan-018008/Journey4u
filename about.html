About Journey4u

Who We Are

Born from a Love of Travel

Journey4u Travel Co. was founded in 2009 by two backpackers who believed that great travel experiences shouldn't be left to chance. What started as a small office in Mumbai has grown into one of India's most trusted travel agencies — with over 12,000 happy travellers and 80+ destinations on our map.

We're not just a booking service. We're storytellers, planners, and fellow travellers who care deeply about giving you a journey worth remembering.
